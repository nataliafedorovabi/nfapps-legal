Privacy Policy

MRR Dashboard by NFapps · Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MRR Dashboard ("the App"), developed by NFapps, handles data when installed on your Stripe account.

1. Data We Access

The App reads the following data from your Stripe account to provide its functionality:

  • Subscription data (amounts, intervals, statuses, dates)
  • Customer names and email addresses
  • Subscription update events

2. How We Use Your Data

All data accessed by the App is used solely to calculate and display MRR metrics within your Stripe Dashboard. We do not store, sell, share, or transmit your Stripe data to any third party, except as described in Section 3.

3. Feedback Feature

The App includes an optional feedback form. If you choose to submit feedback, only the text you enter is transmitted to the App developer via Telegram. No Stripe account data or customer information is included in feedback submissions.

4. Data Storage

The App does not store any of your Stripe data. All data is fetched in real time and displayed only within your browser session. No data is retained after you close the App.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
